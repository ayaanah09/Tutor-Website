Hi, I'm the founder.

Grade 11 Student · Cambridge, ON

I started BrightPath because I noticed a real gap in my neighbourhood. Families who wanted extra support for their kids, and students falling behind through no fault of their own.

I'm a Grade 11 student passionate about computer engineering and problem-solving. Teaching is how I give back, and it's made me a sharper thinker. Every session I run teaches me as much as it teaches them.

Built for Cambridge kids, by one of them.

BrightPath isn't a business. It's a community project. The goal is simple: make sure no kid in our neighbourhood falls behind just because they need a little extra help.

Every session is completely free. We run in community spaces, libraries, and homes around Cambridge. Every session follows the Ontario curriculum so the help actually applies to what's happening in school.
