How It Works

Simple by design.

No complicated onboarding. Just show up, and we'll take care of the rest.

01

Hi, I'm the founder.

Grade 11 Student · Cambridge, ON

I started BrightPath because I noticed a real gap in my neighbourhood — families who couldn't afford tutoring centres, and kids falling behind through no fault of their own.

I'm a Grade 11 student passionate about computer engineering and problem-solving. Teaching is how I give back, and it's made me a sharper thinker. Every session I run teaches me as much as it teaches them.

Built for Cambridge kids, by one of them.

BrightPath isn't a business — it's a community project. The goal is simple: make sure no kid in our neighbourhood falls behind just because their family can't afford extra help.

Sessions are offered at low or no cost for families that need it. We run in community spaces, libraries, and homes around Cambridge. Every session follows the Ontario curriculum so the help actually applies to what's happening in school.
